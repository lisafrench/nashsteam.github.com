Ladies Hack Day (May 11th 2013)

Ladies' Hack day is a day for women of all skill levels and ages to get together and build something radical. If you’ve *never* written a line of code in your life, but you want to learn, we want you here! If you’re a super awesome programmer who knows more about compilers than Grace Hopper, we’re honored to have you. You’ll leave with a fully functioning project that you can call your own. We'll provide coffee, tea, lunch, and cupcakes. You provide your awesome self.

Who we are

Nashville Women's STEAM group is a collective of women in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Math that want to further the community and increase the participation of women.

Why??

We started because we recognized a gap in between the existing community and the women that wish to join it.

We want to create a safe space for women to join these fields that we love so well.

Ethos

These are the philosophy behind our group (borrowed from the Hacker School code of conduct). Please respect them when you attend our events.

  • No Saying "Well, Actually"
  • No Feigning Suprise
  • No Backseat Driving
  • No Subtle Sexism, Racism, Or Any Discrimination
